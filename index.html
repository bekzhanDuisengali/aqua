AQUA HOLDING COMPANY

ABOUT US
Aqua Holding Company is a fully integrated manufacturer that carries out groundwater extraction, bottling and distribution of natural mineral water from the famous Caucasian Mineral Water region of Russia.

The company makes the top two of the Russian bottled water manufacturers.

Aqua Holding Company has two head offices located in Moscow and Essentuki and owns six manufacturing sites in the eco resort region of the Caucasian Mineral Water. Production facilities include six operating and two standby wells and are equipped with state-of-the-art technologies that meet the European standards of quality.

The manufacturing sites are certified on the standard ISO 22 000:2018 by DQS agency.

Aqua Holding Company produces more than 200 million bottles of mineral water every year. We deliver to every region of Russian Federation as well as to 20 countries, including the USA, Canada, China, South Korea and others. We are also looking forward to new directions.

Essentuki

Essentuki is a natural mineral carbonated drinking water and one of the first brands of bottled water in Russia. The production of the legendary Essentuki № 4 and Essentuki № 17 began 150 years ago, and both sub-brands of water instantly became very popular. The Essentuki water remains as natural and healthy to this day as it used to be then.


The City of Essentuki, where we produce the water of the same name, is a famous Russian spa resort with mineral springs, health institutions and 200 years of history. Essentuki springs usually attracted people from all over the country, and the treatment in sanatoriums included drinking mineral water and taking healing baths.

PRODUCTION

The original Essentuki № 4 and Essentuki № 17 are only extracted from the wells of the Essentuki field located at 640 meters above sea level in the Caucasian Mineral Water region.



The Essentuki mineral groundwater deposit is formed by ancient seas and volcanoes over 70 million years ago which determines the unique composition and special healing properties of Essentuki mineral water.


Minerals of the Elbrus subsoil have enriched Essentuki water for more than 800 years. The mineral water is now pumped from wells 600−1000 meters deep.

NARZAN
Narzan is natural mineral drinking water and one of the most famous mineral water brands in Russia. The very first batch of the Narzan bottled mineral water was released back in 1894.
PRODUCTION
The Narzan mineral water comes from the bores of the Kislovodsk deposit in the Caucasian Mineral Waters, a specially protected eco-resort region in Russia. According to geologists, the Narzan spring appeared in the Pliocene, more than 2−3 million years ago. The spring was formed as a result of the mountain-folding process and volcanic activity of the Elbrus stratovolcano in the Caucasus.

To come into being, the Narzan mineral water travels a long way in earth’s womb in the vicinity of Mt. Elbrus. Filtering its way through the rocks, water becomes reach in good minerals and carbon dioxide and then gathers in underground lakes. It comes out through the bore fully preserving its unique natural composition.

GORJI
GORJI is young nature-made volcanic water. A 1-liter bottle of GORJI contains 50% RDI of silicon (Si). Silicon (Si) is a naturally occurring trace mineral and a structural component available in all cells of the body (the Earth’s second most common element after oxygen). It is widely recognized as a vital mineral responsible for metabolic and regeneration processes in the body.
PRODUCTION
GORJI is produced in the Caucasian Mineral Waters resort area known for its healthy environment. Originating from subterranean volcanic processes of the Mount Elbrus, GORJI is called “volcanic”, while rocks dissolve in underground waters to saturate them with invaluable mineral substances, including silicon as a trace mineral of youth and health.

GORJI comes from a 1,502m-deep well that was drilled in 2000 and is therefore thought to be newly-made and young from the hydrogeological perspective. After the water gets to the surface, it is carefully bottled to save it in its original state.

PRODUCTION

The natural composition of the water makes it an ideal choice for maintaining your body’s natural balance.
